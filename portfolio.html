Perspective

This project shows how a stylized building was made step by step. It starts with simple blocky shapes and uses 3-point perspective to make it look 3D. Details, textures, and lines were added to shape the design. The colors follow the 60/30/10 rule mostly one main color, some secondary color, and a small pop of accent color. In the end, lighting and shadows were added to give it depth and a polished look, like something you'd see in a game or concept art.

Man

I made a draft of a man jumping and drew each step of the movement by hand. Then, I put the drawings together to turn it into an animation that shows the jump in motion.
